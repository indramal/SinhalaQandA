Abstract

This research presents an innovative approach to Sinhala language question-answering systems by incorporating visual clues. Our system combines natural language processing techniques with computer vision to enhance the accuracy and contextual understanding of questions in the Sinhala language. This multimodal approach significantly improves the system's ability to comprehend and respond to queries that require visual context interpretation.

Introduction

The development of question-answering systems in Sinhala presents unique challenges due to the language's morphological complexity and limited digital resources. This research addresses these challenges by incorporating visual information processing alongside traditional text-based analysis.

Methodology

Our approach combines:

  • Advanced Natural Language Processing for Sinhala text
  • Computer Vision algorithms for visual clue processing
  • Deep Learning models for multimodal integration
  • Custom dataset creation for Sinhala QA with visual elements

Results

The system demonstrates significant improvements in:

  • Question comprehension accuracy
  • Answer relevance and precision
  • Processing speed and efficiency
  • Handling of complex queries requiring visual context

Conclusion

Our research demonstrates the effectiveness of incorporating visual clues in Sinhala question-answering systems. The multimodal approach significantly enhances the system's ability to understand and respond to complex queries, opening new possibilities for advanced language processing applications in Sinhala.
